Who are we

BEN is a Chinese running group in Greater Boston Area.

Our running group was created in 2012, by a group of Chinese running enthusiasts in Boston. Since then, some of us train hard for races of distances anywhere from a mile to 100 miles. Others simply enjoy daily conversational runs with fellow endorphin addicts.
